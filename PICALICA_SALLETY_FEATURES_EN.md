# Sallety Theme - The Premium Arabic Shopify Theme

**Sallety** is a professional Shopify theme designed specifically to meet the needs of e-commerce store owners in the Middle East. Featuring a modern, attractive design and blazing-fast performance powered by the latest **Tailwind CSS v4**, it's the perfect choice to boost your sales and provide an exceptional user experience.
